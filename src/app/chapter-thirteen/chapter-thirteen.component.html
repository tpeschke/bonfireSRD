ANATOMY of the DIVINE

COVENANTS

Divines have spheres of power that represent their aspects. These are codified into Covenants, or promises that make with their servants in exchange for a portion of their power.

Cults of that Divine typically pick one, maybe two Covenants two of their chosen Divine to focus on.

For instance, the Morrigan, a goddess from the Isles of Yr Alban, watches over Death, War, and Sovereignty. There could be 3 different priests to the Morrigan, one focusing on her role as the Reaper of the Dead, one seeing her as primarily a war goddess, and one worshiping her as the grand judge of the hearts of man.

While they all worship the same Divine, the Miracles they can invoke in her name are different.

This means that priests of different cults may not get along with each other and there can even be intra-faith wars to purge heretics (those that focus on different Covenant from them).

COSMIC LINKS

The Higher Planes are those that exist above the crude and primitive world and so, naturally, the Divines are linked to the events and behaviors of the celestial bodies that make their homes in the heavens.

A Cosmic Link is the specific celestial body that the Divine is associated with and it usually gives an insight to the personality of the god themselves.

COMET This is associated with growing power and cycles. If you roll a 1 on any of your Favor dice, you get 1 Favor back, maximum 1.

EVENING STAR The evening star is a source of strength and beauty: it's the first star to rise in the evening. Miracles invoked, whether beneficial or baleful, reroll a single dice if it rolls a 1. This is non-optional and must be used.

MOON The moon is a steady force, its effects are subtle but constant. Miracles are invoked a total of 7 but half any excess points above that. So, a total of 9 invokes the Miracle but you can only increase the threshold by 1 point.

MORNING STAR The morning star is the last star in the heavens before day and is a lone watcher. You gain 1 Favor for every unanswered prayer.

SUN The sun is the lord of heaven, strong and unbending, its power unquestioned. Miracles are only invoked on a total 15 or higher but any excess points above that are doubled. So, a total of 21 means that priest has 12 additional points to work with.

CYCLICAL Often hailing from older, more primitive times, the Cyclical deity represents earthly inevitabilities in a dichotomy, sometimes with themselves and sometimes with an opposing deity. This can be Spring and Summer, life and death, creation and destruction. They may be weak now, but they will wax unstoppable. For every prayer unanswered, the Priest reduces his Threshold by 1. For every prayer answered, he increases it by 1.

Meaning of the Cosmic Link

The Cosmic Link doesn’t necessarily mean your Patron is primarily the Divine of that Cosmic thing. So, a Divine associated with the moon might not be primarily a moon god, in the same way that while Venus was associated with the Morning Star, she was primarily viewed as a love goddess by the Romans. Still, your holy symbols may incorporate the moon motif commonly to represent him.

FAVOR of the DIVINES

Favor is a measure of your standing with a given god. It’s not an absolute measure, however, but more a measure of the chance that a Divine will answer your prayer. How far your Favor goes ranges widely based on how devoted you are to a god.

ACQUIRING FAVOR

Favor is gained through furthering the Divine’s will in the mortal realm: through holy wars, missionary work, or, most commonly, sacrifices.

When a character gains Favor, he gains a number of points that can be later spent to try and invoke a Miracle.

The baseline to gain Favor for any activity is based on the highest applicable Devotion (either to the cult, the Covenant, or the god) with a roll of 4 or higher netting a Favor.

Favor is unique to the Divine you’re petitioning. Gaining Favor with other Divine, means that you lose an equal amount of Favor with all others because gods are bad at sharing.

SACRIFICES

By giving something to one of the Divine’s cults, you further their material cause. Sacrifices are the most common way to trade in wealth for godly aid.

COMMON These are small, daily sacrifices. These tend to cost about 1 or 2 sc. They can be the sacrifice of a small, sacred animal, the burning of a portion of your meal, or the lighting of incense during morning prayers. They net 1 Favor for Servants of that Divine, while they keep the anointed and unanointed from losing 1 Favor that day.

RITUAL A Ritual sacrifice is accepted only once per 3 months on a predetermined day. These are part of a more elaborate ceremony, normally the crowning event of a day of worship. These sacrifices usually entail the slaughter of a bull, or the first of the flock, possibly even a human sacrifice, but it usually costs about 500 sc. These always net all participants with at least 1d4! Favor, but anointed members gain a chance of gaining an additional 1d6! and Servants of that Divine have a chance of an additional 1d12! Favor.

SACRED These are the zenith of the Divine’s calendar, often ushering in the cult’s New Year. These sacrifices cost 1,000 sc on the low end and are often the closing events of a week long festival. These always net all participants with at least 1d6! Favor, but anointed members have a chance of gaining an additional 1d12! and Servants of that Divine have a chance of an additional 2d12! Favor.

TAKING AN OATH Not a sacrifice parse, Taking an Oath is essentially getting Favor on loan from the Divines for the promise that, if you get out the bad situation you're in, you'll make a huge sacrifice to them soon after. By Taking an Oath, you gain a die worth of Favor for every share of any possible treasure you're about to gain if you live to the Divine. This die is equal to the highest Devotion die related to the Divine you're petitioning if you're Anointed to them. The Unanointed much also promise to become Anointed in addition to the cash and goods, in which case they gain a d8! worth of Favor. This Favor must be spent on a Miracle immediately.

The Divines are extremely litigious. If you don't fulfill your oath in 1 month + 1 for every Honor Category higher than Average Honor you're in, they will seek vengeance. You may no longer gain any Favor with any Divine that shares a pantheon with the one you're in and what you do have will decay, causing you to lose 1 Favor per day. Priests of the Divine will also condemn you as an oath breaker, a very serious accusation, causing you to drop 1 Honor Category per month and preventing you from increasing your Honor. Should you fall into Dishonor, you gain the Excommunicated Flaw.

But I Don’t Have That Kind of Money: Have no fear. Often times cults will have to pool their money to manage these sacrifices. You can simply donate a portion of your money and still gain some benefit, though only the Servant actually officiating gains the Servant bonus: all other priests are simply treated as anointed members.

Now that you’ve got a nice stash of Favor, you probably want to spend it. That’s pretty simple: pray out loud for the Miracle in question. Silent prayers don’t work because no Divine is going to answer a prayer they’re not going to get credit for.

Once you’ve selected a Miracle, you cash in the amount of Favor and roll a die based on your standing. You gain 1 die per Favor spent.

A total of above 10 means that the Miracle happens! To you. Momentarily. In 1 to 12 months.

Fear not, because you can increase that success threshold to change that up after you know the Miracle is going to be invoked. You can’t increase the threshold above what you roll.

PERSONAL (CENTER) The person chosen is the epicenter of the Miracle but is unaffected if they choose not to be. Each additional point increases the Area of Effect around them out 5 ft.

Example of praying for a Miracle: Sigismund the Blessed, a Priest of the Lamb, prayers for a Miracle of Healing for his companion. He cashes in 3 Favor and so rolls 3d20!. He gets a 5, 9, and 15, totaling 29, so the Miracles happens to him for just a moment in 1d12! months.

But he doesn’t want that since his companion is the one bleeding out but he can raise the threshold another 19 points, so changes the Distance to Ear Shot (raising the threshold by 2), and he wants it instantaneously (raising it by 7). He’s got everything he wants, but he can still raise the threshold by another 8 points so he decides to include himself and 1 other companion (raising it by 6). He still have 4 points to spare but he decides it’s not worth it.

MIRACLES OUTSIDE THE DIVINE’S COVENANT

You can always try and invoke a Miracle not associated with your chosen Covenant. In this case, you need to roll a total of 20 or greater to invoke it. Additionally, all increases to the threshold are doubled.

DO I KNOW EXACTLY WHEN A MIRACLE IS COMING?

Short answer: yes. but also, no. Your character knows the time frame of when it will be ready and they will know when it's ready to evoke before that time, they'll have no idea.

Once ready, the supplicant will have a time frame to actually use the miracles power. This time frame is equal to his max Devotion dice in units equal to her chosen Delievery Time. This means that instantaneous miracles need to be used right then and there.

So, for example, a character with a d6 Devotion prayers for a miracle and sets the Delievery Time for 1d4 hours. Her GM rolls a 3. At this point, the character knows the miracle will be ready in the next 1 to 4 hours but is unsure when in that time it'll be. 3 hours pass and the GM signals to the player that the miracle is ready. She may now envoke it at anytime within the next 6 hours.

MIRACLE DESCRIPTIONS

The following are some of the Miracles that you can try and petition your god(s) for in hopes of channeling their power. It is not an exhaustive list and players should feel creative. The rule of thumb is that, as much as possible, the Divine should do as little as possible to achieve the miracle.

The first line is the common name of the Miracle, the second line is the primary Covenants for that Miracle. This is not a definite list - if you can make a good case for why a Miracle belongs in your Covenant, you’re free to do so, but note that while some Covenants seem close (like Nature and Beast), they’re not the same and represent different aspects.

The last line is the effect, or what the Miracle actually does and any exceptions it may contain to the base rules.

ALTER WEATHER

Nature

EFFECT The Divine changes the weather, bringing storms and floods, or intense periods of drought upon the fields of the heathen. This can also keep a period of particularly fair or foul weather in an area.

Rain falls on the righteous and sinner alike and the Divine can’t be bothered to divert floods or aim the storm’s lightning. The supernatural weather affects everyone in the area.

ANOINT

All

PREREQUISITE The initiative must have a Devotion to either the cult, Covenant, or the Divine

EFFECT This is the right of initiation that inducts a heathen into the specific cult of the Divine. Unlike other Miracles, the initiate must be the one to spend the Favor, though if a Servant is officiating, you’ll roll his Favor die.

On a success, the Divine has accepted the sacrifice and shows their approval with a divine omen. From then on, the person is consider anointed. He receives a larger Favor dice when invoking Miracles and, unless it states otherwise, he is completely immune from the ill effects of Miracles invoked by priests of his cult. His Devotion die to either the cult, Covenant, or Divine is increased 1 size permanently.

A person can only be anointed to a single cult. If they decide to renounce their faith they gain the Flaw Excommunicated. If they wish to shift to a different cult of the same Divine, they’ll need to be re-Anointed and drop an Honor category. If they’re converting to a completely new faith (a new Divine), they drop two.

AUGER DEAD

Death | Dreams

EFFECT By offering up sacraments to the Divine, they may allow you to bring back the dead for a short time so a person can talk to it.

The priests may ask the spirit questions but it doesn’t gain any unnatural knowledge and, in fact, it’s knowledge of the afterlife is fairly foggy as well. So, if a person was killed by a blow to the back of the head, they won’t know who their attacker was.

BLESS

Adversity | Beauty | Fate | Fire | Home | Honor

EFFECT This gives a bonus of 1 to the next dice roll. The Divine strength of this Miracle can be increased by raising the threshold by the bonus amount. So, a +4 to a dice roll would raise that threshold by 4.

BLESSING of PROTECTION

Adversity | Battle | Beauty | Fate | Fire | Home | Honor

EFFECT This endows the worshiper with godly protection, allowing otherwise normal skin to turn aside the blows of the infidel. Blessing of Protection increases the DR of anyone affected by the Miracle by 1. This holy endowment can be increased by raising the threshold by the bonus times itself. So, a +3 to a DR would raise that threshold by 9.

CHARM ANIMAL

Beasts | Nature

EFFECT By the exertion of the divine will over a lesser creature, the deity temporarily links it with the soul of supplicant, allowing them to control it as if it was perfectly domesticated.

It doesn’t bestow the animal with supernatural intelligence, so don’t expect your new pet crow to be able to pick a lock but it will perfectly understand that you want it to pick a lock.

After the duration is over, prey will simply retreat into nature while predators will go berserker and try and get revenge if they’re around their old master.

COMMAND

Beauty | Deception | Fate | Home | Honor

EFFECT Few can resist the will of the gods. Even the heathen bends the knee at the unfurled power of the heavens. Any one up to an Area of Effect of Earshot is affected by the divine commandment spoken by the faithful and tries their best to comply.

This command needs to be simple and something that the targets has control over, as well as something they can accomplish in a few seconds. So, “Stop” halts the target, while “Die” does nothing as the target doesn’t control that.

Increasing the duration simply expands in the command, however, the action needs to still be simple. “Push that button” works, for example, while “Do a dance” will result in a quick step ball-change and that’s it.

Consider what is the least the target can do and still technically follow the command.

CONFOUND

Adversity | Beauty | Deception

EFFECT By means of channeling the seraphic presence, the servant can cause the infidels to shudder for a moment. This miracle causes anyone in the area to delay their action by 1d4! seconds or have to roll an additional dice of 1d4! while doing a skill check.

This benediction can be empowered by raising the threshold by a number equal to the amount of dice size increases times itself. So, increasing to a 1d8! would raise that threshold by 4.

CURSE

Adversity | Dreams | Disease

EFFECT The horrors of the other world can shrink and break the feeble minds of mortals by unleashing a portion of it on them.

By means of this Miracle, the cleric can temporarily inflict a Flaw from the Mental Flaws table on the heathen and pagan.

This is randomly rolled unless the servant doubles the overall threshold of the Miracle, in which case, they may choose.

DISGUISE

Beauty | Deception

EFFECT This shrouds an object or person in a holy glamour, changing their appearance. This Miracle, however, only changes superficial appearances, such as looks, race, or height but not tactile things such as texture or weight.

Only by doubling the total overall threshold of this Miracle will the Divine change those things.

DIVINE CALL

Adversity | Dreams | Fate | Fire | Honor

EFFECT This binds the targets to a holy geas to serve the Divine on a quest or crusade.

While on this quest, the endowed crusaders lose 1 Honor per session they don’t actively pursue their divine mandate.

Of course, the servants of the gods can’t really be bothered to view things so closely so what “actively pursue” means enforced in a more letter rather than spirit of the law.

Additionally, if they do pursue their deity's crusade, they gain 1 Favor every session.

DIVINE CHARISMA

Beauty | Fire | Home | Honor

EFFECT The gods have an unearthly grace and poise, their charm irresistible to mortals.

By means of this Miracle, the servant is gifted with an additional 3d6! dice to use in a Social Conflict.

This benediction can be empowered by raising the threshold by a number equal to the amount of dice size increases times itself. So, invoking a 3d8! would raise that threshold by 4.

DRAIN ENERGY

Adversity | Beasts | Death | Disease | Nature

EFFECT The mortal coil finds itself weak and impotent in the face of the eternal, eager to give up in its sight.

This Miracle inflicts 1 level of Fatigue per second to everyone in the Area of Effect.

This Fatigue is healed through normal means.

EARTH GRIP

Adversity | Death

EFFECT The Middle Realm may protest the will of the Higher Powers but even it must come when they call. This Miracle causes the ground to reach up, grappling and restraining pagans in the Area of Effect, preventing them from moving and effectively defending themselves, defending with a d6!.

EARTHQUAKE

Nature

EFFECT The earthquake is the epitome of the Divine flexing its power on the Middle Realm. Nothing invokes the fear of god like the solid ground you’re standing on suddenly heaving.

This delays all actions, both those of the faithful and the pagan, for the length of the Miracle.

Additionally, earthquakes, especially localized ones, have the added benefit of destroying buildings and fortifications, or providing landslides in particularly treacherous areas. This effect should be left up to the GM for the exact specifics of how it’s applied.

EXALT CHAMPION

Adversity | Battle | Beasts | Fate | Fire | Guardians | Honor

EFFECT This gives a bonus of 1 to a dice roll of the champion’s choosing within the allotted duration. The Divine strength of this Miracle can be increased by raising the threshold by the bonus times itself. So, a +4 to a dice roll would raise that threshold by 16.

This blessing may be invoked after a dice is rolled and the result is given.

EXORCISM

Dreams | Fire | Home

EFFECT This strips the Miracle of another Divine from their faithful, leaving them to fend for their own. The Exorcism must be at least equal in Duration to the Duration that’s left in the Miracle it’s removing, otherwise, even if the total Favor is above the threshold needed, it fails completely.

GODSPEED

Fire | Nature

EFFECT All matter is the domain of the Divines, the spirits are linked with physical substance.

By this Miracle, the Divine consents in letting their supplicant use the holy pathways and travel between objects associated with the Divine within the Distance.

The entrance and exit in question must be large enough to completely cover the supplicant, so you can’t simply jump into and out of every torch in the area.

GUIDANCE

Dreams | Fate | Guardians | Home | Honor

EFFECT While the deity in question may have an entire cosmos to run, they can sometimes condescend to answer the prayers of the faithful.

The supplicant gets to answer a single question equal the number of times they increase the Duration. So, increasing the threshold by 4 (2 increases), nets 2 questions answered.

However, the Divine (or more likely a low level seraphic clerk) has a lot to do so the answers, while complete, can come off as obtuse, with inconvenient pronouns dropped randomly and a lot of missing context.

Most clerics using this Miracle use their additional questions to gain clarity on one subject or event.

HAMPER MAGIC

Death | Deception | Dreams | Earth | Home

EFFECT What the heathen magic-users forget is that the Painted World is first the property of the Divines and that they’re connection to it is only at the Divines’ behest.

This Miracle reminds them of this fact. Within the Area of Effect, all magic-users reduce their channeling die by 1 size. This sacred barrier can be strengthened by raising the threshold by the bonus times itself. So, reducing that dice by 3 sizes raises that threshold by 9.

If this would reduce the channeling die to below a d4!, the magic-user has completely lost their connection to the Painted World and is unable to gain SPs in the area.

HEAL

Battle | Nature | Guardians | Home

EFFECT The mortal flesh is a deteriorating prison, who’s finally seconds are known only to those on high. It is to man, elves, and orcs to try to use that time wisely and hope that those above grant them more sands for their hourglass.

This Miracle heals any one in the Area of Effect by a Wound Categories worth of Health or rids them of one auxiliary effect or regrows one lost body part.

This holy boon can be strengthened, allowing those affected to pick another healing bonus, raising the threshold by the number of bonuses times itself. So, healing 2 Wound Categories and regrowing an arm (3 boons) raises the threshold by 9.

The Duration of this Miracle can’t be increased.

HOLY GRACE

Beauty | Fate | Honor

EFFECT Through the blessing of the gods, the faithful can rise above their natural abilities and seize a higher standard of living, if only temporarily.

Imbued with Holy Grace, anyone affected by this Miracle raises 1 of their ability scores by 1. This holy endowment can be increased by raising the threshold by the bonus times itself. So, a +3 to a single stat would raise that threshold by 9.

HOLY of HOLIES

Battle | Beasts | Death | Guardians | Home

EFFECT Where the Divines tread, men fear to walk. This sanctifies the ground in the Area of Effect, making it impossible for the unanointed of that cult to enter or leave the area, depending on the supplicant’s will.

IMBUE WITH FATE

Battle | Dreams | Fate | Guardians | Honor

EFFECT The Honorable have special missions set aside by the Divines, and to this end, they’re often gift their favorites with extra luck.

This Miracle allows those affected to redeem a single bonus from their Honor within the Duration without dropping that Honor Category, including Divine Intervention.

The number of bonuses redeemed for free can be increased by 1 by doubling the Threshold.

INFLICT STRESS

Adversity | Death | Disease

EFFECT The minds of the world are nothing more than clay in the hands of the gods, meant to be shaped and reformed at their will.

This Miracle inflicts 1 Stress per second to everyone in the Area of Effect of the spell.

This Stress is healed through normal means.

INVEST OBJECT

Battle | Beauty | Fate | Honor

EFFECT By power and great glory the will of the Divines is disseminated upon their servants and by the tools of their servants is their will enforced.

This gives a bonus of +1 to every metric of an item, so investing a weapon gives it a bonus of 1 to it’s Attack, Damage, Speed, Defense, and Measure, for the Duration of the Miracle.

Additionally, this item gains a halo of holy power marking it as an instrument of the gods, increasing it’s Social Die size by 1 as well.

The seraphic power invested in the object can be increased by raising the threshold by the bonus times itself. So, granting a shield a +3 to its DR, Defense, Speed Defense, Cover, Fatigue Adjustment, Shield Breakage, and Social Die size would raise that threshold by 9.

INVOKE EMOTION

Beauty | Deception

EFFECT A holy glamour can shroud the minds of even the strongest of minds, giving the servant total control of another’s emotions.

This Miracles either changes the mood of everyone in the Area of Effect (those anointed included) or keeps them all the same.

This has the natural advantage in situations of diplomacy and so it nets the servant a d6! in a Social Conflict. This Miracle can be empowered by raising the threshold by the number of dice size increases times itself. So, adding a d12! to your dice pool (3 increases) bumps your threshold up by 9.

MANA

Home | Nature

EFFECT Like the dew of heaven, the Divine’s grace is not just spiritual in nature but also can relieve the suffering of more temporal matters

This causes a soft bread to form either in the hands of the supplicant or appear in the surrounding area depending on the amount requested.

This mana holds everything a mortal requires to survive if eaten, but rots the next day (unless it’s a holy day for the cult).

The base amount miraclely produced is enough to feed a single person for a day, but that number can be increased by raising the threshold by the number of people times itself. So, feeding 5 people for a single day raising that threshold by 25.

MARTYR

Adversity | Death | Deception | Fate | Home | Guardians

EFFECT The life of a single servant is meaningless if it can be given to further the mission of the Divine in the Middle Realm.

With this Miracle, all damage dealt to a single person is dealt to another instead.

The two selected people must be anointed members of the same cult within the Area of Effect but their link continues for the Duration, even if they leave the initial area.

MIGHT of DEITY

Battle | Earth | Nature | Honor

EFFECT By the might of the Divine, those lesser beings fall to their knees or are unceremoniously pushed back.

This Miracle causes everyone within the Area of Effect to be suffer a Knock Back as if pushed by a hostile wind. The epicenter is selected by the supplicant. This distance can be increased by raising the threshold by the number of Knock-Backs they suffer times itself. So, a Triple Knock-Back would would raise that threshold by 9.

On top of this, you may throw everyone to the ground, knocking them prone, in addition to the distance traveled by doubling the overall threshold total.

The Might of Deity can also be used to move objects, such as houses or mountains, but in these cases it doesn’t affect anyone else and the object only moves up to 5 ft increments. If you’re moving something larger than a person, double the threshold for every size category above Medium the object is.

MORTAL INVIGORATION

Adversity | Battle | Fate

EFFECT The body is just clay operated by the spirit, which the realm and domain of the Divines. Simply touching these spirits can put strength back in exhausted muscles and bones.

This Miracles removes 1 level of Fatigue from any affected and keeps them from gaining further Fatigue for the Duration.

The amount of Fatigue healed can be increased by raising the Threshold by the amount increased times itself. So healing 3 Fatigue would raise the Threshold by 9 (3 x 3).

OBSCURE SENSE

Adversity | Beauty | Death | Deception

EFFECT The senses are the handicaps of the Middle Realm, forcing you to believe exactly what you see. The Divines would remind you that the highest truths are those unseen.

This Miracle inflicts either blindness or deafness or muteness on anyone within the Area of Effect, as if they had the corresponding Flaw.

The supplicant may wish for the Divine to use this Miracle to hide an object. In this case, those in the Area of Effect become blind and deaf to that specific object.

PART SUBSTANCE

Nature

EFFECT By this Miracle, the Divine clears the path before his servants. Nothing stands in the way of their sacred mission.

This Miracle temporarily splits a land feature within the Area of Effect, so if it is a mountain, it creates a valley, if a river, the chosen people are able to pass through on dry land.

PILLAR of FIRE

Fate | Fire | Honor

EFFECT This conjures a column of shadow and smoke by day and pillar of fire by night that serves as a guiding light for the faithful on their holy pilgrimage.

The pillar moves in the direction of what the supplicant is seeking, showing them the way for a long as it lasts and standing guard over them at night.

Increasing the Duration of this Miracle is done so at half cost, but it can’t be permanent.

PREDICT WEATHER

Dreams | Nature

EFFECT The gods are privy to what men cannot know but by laying petitions at the altars of the world, they may bestow this heavenly knowledge on their chosen servants.

By means of this Miracle, the supplicant gains a knowledge of any weather changes and what they are in the Area of Effect during the Duration.

RELAX

Beauty | Dreams | Home

This Miracle heals 1 Stress per second to all the anointed in the Area of Effect.

SCRY

Adversity | Beauty | Dreams

SOOTHING AURA

Adversity | Battle | Fate

SUMMON OBJECT

Battle | Fate | Guardians | Home

EFFECT Crude matter is easily shaped and brought into being by the Divines, allowing them to arm and equip their people in times of needs and distress.

TALK to ANIMALS

Beasts

EFFECT There is a secret language weaved into the fabric of all living spirits that allows them to hear the call of their gods.

With this Miracle, the supplicant is granted the ability to effortlessly communicate with the creatures that were initially in the Area of Effect when the prayer was answered.

This does not, however, give the animals supernatural knowledge, but they can relate things they’ve heard, seen, or smelled if questioned by the servant of the Divine.

TALK to PLANTS

Nature

EFFECT There is a secret language weaved into the fabric of all living spirits that allow them to hear the call of their gods.

This Miracle allows the supplicant to converse with all living plant matter within the Area of Effect, both hearing their words and having their words heard and understood.

This doesn’t give the plants supernatural knowledge, but they are able to communicate their thoughts and experiences in an understandable method, though they pay more attention to the spiritual description of people, events, and things, rather than physical ones, as such, their answers are often helpful, if not a bit obtuse.

TRANSFORM

Nature

EFFECT The nature of reality, as firm as the dwarven lords might tell you it is, is shaky; the spirit almost wishing to be twisted and reforged into other things.

This Miracle causes a thing, completely within the Area of Effect, to change its physical form. This could be a simple transformation, turning water into wine, or something more complicated, like a staff to a snake.

When shifting live matter to dead matter, or vice versa, you must double the threshold for this Miracle. Making something that was yet living into an animate object doesn’t kill it, and it returns to normal health after the Duration is finished.

TRAUMATIZE

Adversity | Battle

EFFECT It is a simple thing for one of such cosmic strength to touch the body of a mortal and there wreak unspeakable havoc, twisting the corporal form until all it knows is pain.

This forces everyone in the Area of Effect to make a Trauma check vs double their Con score.

This can be lowered in increments of half their Con but each time, it doubles the total threshold. So, getting the check down to their Con would cause the threshold to be doubled twice.

This Miracle may not have its Duration increased.

VEIL OF LIGHT/DARK

Deception

EFFECT This creates a veil of shadow or pall of light that darkens or illuminates the Area of Effect, bringing it down to dim lighting or up from total darkness to dim lighting.

You may also use this Miracle to jump from total darkness to daylight or vice versa but doing so doubles the Miracle’s threshold.

WATER FROM ROCK

Earth | Nature

EFFECT Like the dews of heaven, the Divine’s grace is not just spiritual in nature but it can also relieve the suffering of more temporal matters.

This causes a stream of fresh water to burst from a rock within the Area of Effect.

The base amount miraculously produced is enough to slate the thirst of a single person for a day, but that number can be increased by raising the threshold by the number of people times itself. So, producing enough water for 5 people for a single day raises that threshold by 25.

ZONE of TRUTH

Dreams | Fate | Guardians | Home | Honor

EFFECT In the Area of Effect of this Miracle, the Divines have permitted no falsehoods to reign, at least, no known ones to reign.

The Zone of Truth forces anyone in it to, when questioned, relate the truth as far as they know it. They can however, get around it by either not consciously knowing what they’re saying is a lie or if they answer with a half-truth, however, that nets them a point of Stress for each answer given that way.
