Archetypes represent your character’s background training and starting skills. Your character’s class is a gestalt of two Archetypes of your choosing.

Archetypes are divided into Archetype Categories: Expert, Magical, Servant, and Warrior. These represent the overall theme of the individual Archetype. For instance, Warrior Archetypes tend to be good at things related to combat. So, if you’re looking to make a combat focused character, you can look there.

You choose a Primary and a Secondary Archetype and pay the price in CrP for both based on your race.

You use the Health Die, the Skills & Masteries, and the Primary Drawback sections from the Primary, and combine both Archetype Abilities section.

Each Archetype also has a Primary Boon, which modifies the Archetype Abilities of your Primary and is based on the Secondary's Archetype category.

While you may want to double down into the exact same Archetype but Magical Archetypes are the only ones that you can do that with. You may choose two Archetypes from the same Category, however.

Your GM may choose to reject a Class based on the fact that the two Archetypes don’t fit together thematically. So, while a Thief/Priest (Beast Covenant) might be really powerful because of the Beast Covenant’s Shape Changing ability, if there’s not a Patron of Thieves and Animals in the campaign world, you’re out of luck.

Expert Archetypes are those that focus on real-world non-combat skills, as opposed to relying on the Divines or magic as a source of strength.

Expert Archetypes tend to rely on Intellect, Wisdom, and Charisma, primarily.

ADVENTURER

Adventures gain the most free skills of all Archetypes allowing for the largest breathe of knowledge, however, they’re cheaper skills. They can also help others, giving them a chance to try again at Skill Checks they fail.

HEALTH DIE

d8

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Adventurers receive 2 free purchases of any two different, non-Lore Skill Suites.

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

SKILL FOCUS (Any < 6 CrP, 7) At first level and every level up, Adventurers receive 7 free skill purchases. These can be distributed between any Advanced Skills under 6 CrP in cost (before Skill Adept) within either of the two Skill Suites he picked at first level. Purchases may not be doubled up on in the same level.

Adventurer As A Secondary

If you choose Adventurer as your Secondary Archetype, you’ll still select 2 non-Lore Skill Suites to pull from, however, if your Primary also has a Skill Focus, you can’t pick that Skill Focus’s Skill Suite.

HELPING HAND The Adventurer is not just about knowing things but also giving others a helping hand in times of need. The Adventurer may allow another person in the party to reroll 1 non-Lore Skill Check per session at first level and an additional time for every 4 levels obtained after that.

SKILL ADEPT This character has a knack for skills. All Advanced Skills have their cost reduced by 1 CrP. If that would reduce a cost to 0, this character instead gets two purchases of the skill for 1 CrP. Additionally, they gain a 10% discount to any Skill Suites they have a Skill Focus for.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Increase the number of free skills from the Adventurer’s Skill Focus by 2.

MAGICAL Helping Hand lowers to every 3 levels.

SERVANT Increase the number of free skills from the Adventurer’s Skill Focus by 2.

WARRIOR Increase the number of free skills from the Adventurer’s Skill Focus by 2.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

Adventurers tend to eschew book smarts for street smarts: the Lore Skill Suite and any Lore Advanced Skills cost double.

DIPLOMAT

The Diplomat is a strong willed character that can rephrase his arguments carefully so that they have the most impact when trying to convince someone of something. They also increase the strength of character, making them less likely to suffer long term problems from Social Conflicts.

HEALTH DIE

d4

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Diplomats receive 1 free purchase of the Streetwise Skill Suite

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

CAREFUL WORDING A Diplomat has the grace and tact to be able to reword an argument in the most effective way possible. For every 5 levels obtained, the Diplomat receives 1 free reroll of one of the dice in their dice pool per escalation in Social Conflict. They take the higher of the two results.

STRONG PERSONALITY As a Diplomat grows, she becomes more confident in who she is and what she wants. At 2nd level and every 3rd level after that, a Diplomat can increase the strength of one of their Traits by 1 dice (up to a d20) or they save them and cash in two of these increases for a new weak Trait starting at 1d4.

RESILIENT CHARACTER Surprisingly, those with the strongest personalities have the greatest power to resist abuse. A Diplomat’s threshold for Repercussions from Social Conflicts is increased by 2, it's 10 - their current Fatigue that they have to roll over.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Careful Wording lowers to every 4 levels and Strong Personality lowers to every every other level.

MAGICAL Strong Personality lowers to every other levels.

SERVANT Resilient Character increase Repercussion threshold to 3.

WARRIOR Strong Personality lowered to every other level.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

Diplomats put their reputation on the table whenever they engage in Social Conflict. They drop an Honor category whenever they lose a Conflict.

NOBLE

🜂 Advanced Rule

The Noble is typically aloof and hard to impress and so can convince people simply by making them unsure about their conviction, shrinking their dice in Social Conflict.

HEALTH DIE

d4

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Nobles receive 1 free purchase of the Tactics Skill Suite

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

NOBLE BEARING By reminding others of their lower station in life, you can remind them just how petty their needs are. Once per escalation, you can lower all the dice for one characteristic by 1 size for every enemy in the Conflict. If this would drop it below a d4, that dice is removed from the pool. Anyone who’s had their dice size reduced in this way may increase it backup for 1 Stress per increase to it’s normal size.

You may use this ability an additional time per escalation for every 3 levels you’ve obtained.

This ability doesn’t work for Leverage.

ROYAL BACKGROUND Nobles form an elite class of society, giving them a strong sense of self and confidence. Their Devotion to their clan starts at a 1d10 and they gain the Trait “Noble Heritage” at 3d6 for free. This doesn't replace any of their 6 free Traits and may be increased in the normal matter.

INHERITANCE This character may have had an extra leg up in life, allowing them the opportunity to start with better equipment than others. They gain a free weapon of their choice and also gain 20 sc for each CrP cashed in at the end of Character Creation.

If they’re paired with another Archetype with the Inheritance ability, they may opt for a weapon and shield, two weapons, or 1 suit of armor. Additionally, they gain 30 sc for each CrP cashed in, instead of the standard 20 sc.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Noble Bearing is increased to every level.

MAGICAL Noble Bearing is increased to every 2 levels.

SERVANT Noble Bearing is increased to every level.

WARRIOR Noble Bearing is increased to every level.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

Nobles are supposed to be exemplars of Honor and so they’re held to a higher standard: they gain the penalties for Dishonor when they’re in Low Honor. They still receive the bonus, however.

RANGER

The Ranger’s strengths rely mostly in their survival skills, making them excellent to have in the wilderness but they also gain a bonus to their movement that allows them to out pace others during skirmishes.

d8

Rangers receive 1 free purchase of the Survival Skill Suite.

ARCHETYPES ABILITIES

QUICK FOOTED The Ranger relies on being able to cross great swaths of land quickly, chasing down their prey. At first level and for every 4 levels he’s obtained, the Ranger increases his base movement rate by 1 foot. So, a Stroll becomes 3.5 ft/second, a Walk 7 ft/second, and Jog 14 ft/sec and so on.

SKILL FOCUS (Survival, 3) At first level and every level up, Rangers receive 3 free skill purchases. These can be distributed between any Advanced Skills from the Survival Skill Suite. Purchases may not be doubled up on in the same level.

SKILL ADEPT This character has a knack for skills. All Advanced Skills have their cost reduced by 1 CrP. If that would reduce a cost to 0, this character instead gets two purchases of the skill for 1 CrP. Additionally, they gain a 10% discount to any Skill Suites they have a Skill Focus for.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Increase the number of free skills from the Ranger’s Skill Focus by 2.

MAGICAL Increase the number of free skills from Adventurer’s Skill Focus by 2.

SERVANT Increase the number of free skills from Adventurer’s Skill Focus by 2.

WARRIOR Lower the number of free skills from Adventurer’s Skill Focus by 2.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

A Ranger finds armor restricting and irritating: all Armor Masteries are 2 CrP more expensive per Tier (Tier 1: 10 CrP, Tier 3: 22 CrP).

SAGE

The Sage gets some of the most expensive and cerebral skills for free but also, because of his intense training, he has the ability to instantly reroll any skill he chooses.

MEMORY TRAINING The Sage’s training doesn’t just revolve around studying but also on how to study, allowing them better recall of information than those without such mental discipline. The Sage may reroll 1 Skill Check per session at first level and an additional time for every 4 levels obtained after that.

SKILL FOCUS (lore, 3) At first level and every level up, Sages receive 3 free skill purchases. These can be distributed between any Advanced Skill from the Lore Skill Suite. Purchases may not be doubled up on in the same level.

SKILL ADEPT This character has a knack for skills. All Advanced Skills have their cost reduced by 1 CrP. If that would reduce a cost to 0, this character instead gets two purchases of the skill for 1 CrP. Additionally, they gain a 10% discount to any Skill Suites they have a Skill Focus for.

MAGICAL Increase Skill Focus to 4 free skills.

SERVANT Increase Skill Focus to 4 free skills or lower Memory Training to every 3 levels.

WARRIOR lower Memory Training to every 3 levels.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

The Sage has spent most of his time buried in study and so may take a bit longer to react to reality: they always roll an initiative die 2 sizes larger.

Though much study and natural talent, Magical Archetypes can channel the Painted World, using its power to alter reality.

Magical Archetypes rely more on clever applications of spells rather than outright doing damage and so can often subtly shift the playing field in their favor.

RUNEGALDER

Unlike other magic-users, the Runegalder seals magical power in his bond, allowing him to not risk his body when working with supernatural forces. This allows him to cast quickly and consistently but limits the amount and flexibility of his spells.

HEALTH DIE

d6

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Runegalders receive 1 free purchase of the Lore Skill Suite as well as an additional free purchase of Literacy in their native language and the Arcana skill.

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

VANCIAN CASTING The Runegalder chains the power of spells to various runes that he then invests, sealing them with his blood until he’s ready to release them.

Every 24 hours, the Runegalder can seal a number of spell equal to 1 + his Min Spell Point from his Wisdom. He can also bind an additional rune for every 4 levels he’s obtained. This means that he may cast those spells at any time by simply speaking the rune they’re linked to, once he's prepared them.

Speaking the rune releases it from it’s binding so speaking the same rune again before he’s resealed it will not recast the spell - the spell’s power is gone.

He may cast a spell multiple times per day but he’ll need to bind each instance of the spell to a different rune

Speaking a rune in this way doesn’t affect the Runegalder and so he gains no Fatigue by casting like other magic users.

He may also unseal a rune without actually casting it but he not able to immediately reseal a new rune right after.

GRIMOIRE This is the Runegalder’s spell book and keeps the spells that he binds to runes. Unlike a normal spell book, however, the spells are scribed in meteorite ink and blood that seals their magic power in the text itself. Essentially, the Runegalder’s Grimoire does the channeling latently for him.

This Grimoire starts with 3 random spells of 1st Degree and gains an additional spell every 3rd level. New spell’s have a Degree equal to the Runegalder’s level divided by 3, rounded up.

A spell has a number of SP equal to 10 x its Degree that are invested in it when it is scribed into the Grimoire. These SP cannot be redistributed and changed: spells in the Grimoire are immutable.

So, a Runegalder scribes Disorient as a 2nd Degree spell. He gains 20 SP. He chooses to make it a Sphere spell (3 SP), to increase its Duration up to 5 Seconds (16 SP), and increase the Saving Throw by 1 (1 SP). Now, whenever he speaks a rune seal to that spell, he casts it with an Area of Effect of a Sphere, a 5 second Duration, and +1 to his Saving Throw roll.

Spells inscribed at different Degrees count as completely different spells and must be sealed to different runes. So if the Runegalder has a 1st Degree Flash Grenade and a 5th Degree Flash Grenade, he would need two runes to cast both. He couldn’t simply bind “Flash Grenade” to one rune and then choose which he wanted to use in the moment.

SKILL FOCUS (Arcana, 1) Through the course of her career, a Runegalder seeks to further their knowledge of the occult. They receive 1 free purchase of the Arcana Advanced Skill at every level up.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Runes can be resealed every 18 hours. They gain a new spell every 3nd level.

MAGICAL The only magical secondary archetype available for this archetype is the Runegalder. See the Runegalder Doubling Down Benefits Sidebar below.

SERVANT Runes can be resealed every 18 hours.

WARRIOR Runes can be resealed every 18 hours. They gain a new spell every 3nd level.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

The Runegalder uses his body as a prison for latent magical energies which wears on a person over time. He ages at twice the normal rate.

Additionally, the Runegalder's magic is 'dead' and so is less potent than other types of magic, thus allowing some to shrug off their spells.

When casting a spell, the Runegalder rolls a d20! + 15 + their level. All those that would be affected by the spell roll a d20! + their level. If their total is above the Runegalder's, they're unaffected by the his magic. This roll is made regardless of whether or not the person wants to be affected by the spell.

Runegalder Doubling Down Benefits

VANCIAN CASTING

The Runegalder may rebind his runes every 12 hours.

He can also bind one spell for every 2 levels he’s obtained, rounded up.

GRIMOIRE

The Grimoire starts with 6 scribed spells and gains a new one every level.

Additionally, they don’t have to roll a new spell and instead may choose to select a spell they already know and inscribe it anew. If the spell is the same Degree as the copy, it counts as a new spell and may have its SP invested differently. This means that you can have two 1st Degree Earth Shifts that have different Area of Effects, for example.

Choosing to ‘upgrade’ a spell in this way doesn’t destroy the original.

Servant Archetypes represent those that have devoted themselves to higher causes, lifting their eyes from the Middle Realm to a more pure order, whether that’s the Divines, the Ancestral Spirits, or the Bones of Ymir.

Servant Archetypes play the best with others, often modifying the other Archetype’s powers with Divine assistance.

PRIEST

Priests focus on casting Miracles on other people rather than themselves, making them a good support Archetype for other classes. Their Miracles vary widely based on their patron Divine.

HEALTH DIE

d6

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Priests receives 3 purchases of the Religion (The Priest’s Covenant) Advanced Skill for free

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

DIVINE COVENANT During Character Creation, a Priest selects 1 Covenant and 1 Cosmic Link, representing their diety’s sphere of influence and personality, respectively.

Covenants change which Miracles the Priest has access to while Cosmic Links change the ways you gain and spend Favor.

SHEPHERD OF THE PEOPLE If some else prayers for a Miracle within site of the Priest, the Priest can spend his own Favor to increase the effective cost of that Miracle. This bonus is subtracted from the Priest’s current Favor, not the supplicant’s. This ability doesn’t work on those Anointed into other cults.

He gains half, rounded down, of this Favor back, regardless of whether or not the prayer was heard.

FAVORED OF THE DIVINES The Priest’s mission isn’t a selfish one, but it does have some benefits and they’re loved for their devotion to their Divines beyond others. They add 1/2 their level (rounded down) to their Max Favor..

CATECHISM By studying the holy texts, the Priest is about to discern the will of the Divines and better understand the Higher Realms. They receive a free purchase of the Religion (Divines) Advanced Skill at every level up.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Favored of the Divines becomes + level.

MAGICAL Favored of the Divines becomes + 1/2 level, rounded up.

SERVANT Priests can’t select another Servant Archetype as their Secondary.

WARRIOR Favored of the Divines becomes + level.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

A Priest has sworn themselves to a Higher Power and as a token of their Covenant, they must select a taboo; an action or thing they must never do, such as cut their hair or eat meat. This must be appropriate to the Divine and be approved by the GM before play begins.

War is a ladder by which capable people are able to climb the ranks of society. Warrior Archetypes focus on combat and battle, either by training and discipline or by out maneuvering their opponent.

Unsurprisingly, these Archetypes focus on Constitution, but how you approach combat may decide whether you favor Strength, Dexterity, or even Intellect or Wisdom.

CATERAN

The Cateran is all about flanking your opponent and keeping other fighters locked down. As such, they work best with other fighters.

HEALTH DIE

d8

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Caterans receives the Deadly Flanker Mastery, as well as free Light Armor Training and 1 Weapon Training.

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

BABY-SITTING While an individual Cateran may vary in his ability to take care of infants, they all share the ability to keep combatants locked down.

Whenever a person engaged with the Cateran tries to disengage, the Cateran gets an immediate free attack that, if it hits, deals half damage. This happens regardless of where they are on the Clock and doesn’t effect when their next attack happens.

CLOSING OPENINGS Flanking is the art of dividing people’s attention and the Cateran has perfected that art. Anyone flanked by a Cateran lowers their attack die by 1 size, regardless of who they’re attacking.

SHADOWING By keeping close to their opponent, the Cateran can negate people’s defensive posturing.

Once at first level and an additional time for every 4 levels they obtain, a Cateran can negate any bonuses an opponent gets from moving, whether that’s shifting, stepping back, or whatever.

This also stops their opponent from moving.

BUM RUSH A Cateran can also use his allies to more effectively flank their opponents. Once at first level and an additional time for every 7 levels they’ve obtained, the Cateran can give another ally flanking the same enemy the benefits of the Deadly Flank Mastery if they don’t have it.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Shadowing is reduced to every 2 levels and Bum Rush is reduced to every 5 levels

MAGICAL Shadowing is reduced to every 3 levels

SERVANT Shadowing is reduced to every 2 levels

WARRIOR Shadowing is reduced to every 2 levels and Bum Rush is reduced to every 4 levels

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

The Cateran is a group fighter and fighting alone can be unnerving for him. When engaged with enemies alone and not flanking, the Cateran is unable to use his Honor bonuses, though he is still penalized if his Honor is low enough.

KNIGHT

The Knight focuses on shield and armor, and with their larger Health Die, they tend to be pretty tanky. Additionally, their Inheritance allows them to get the more expense weapons for free.

HEALTH DIE

d12

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Knights receive 1 free purchase of the Tactics Skill Suite.

Knights receive the Armor Training (Heavy), Shield Training, and 1 Weapon Training Mastery for free.

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

IMPROVED MASTERIES A Knight is a master of armor and defense, spending years conditioning himself to be able to wear armor and use a shield without breaking a sweat. Knights can purchase all Tiers of Armor and Shield Masteries for half cost.

INHERITANCE This character may have had an extra leg up in life, allowing them the opportunity to start with better equipment than others. They gain a free weapon of their choice and also gain 20 sc for each CrP cashed in at the end of Character Creation.

If they’re paired with another Archetype with the Inheritance ability, they may opt for a weapon and shield, two weapons, or 1 suit of armor. Additionally, they gain 30 sc for each CrP cashed in, instead of the standard 20 sc.

WARRIOR TRAINING A Knight receives a free Tier 3 Armor or Shield Mastery for every 5 levels she’s obtained.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Warrior Training lowered to every 3 levels.

MAGICAL Warrior Training is lowered to every 4 levels.

SERVANT Warrior Training lowered to every 2 levels.

WARRIOR Warrior Training lowered to every 2 levels.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

The Knight is a creature of Honor and respects Honor where they find it. They may be killers but they have a code, whether articulated or not. A Knight may never use a long ranged weapon on a foe that has greater than Average Honor.

SOLDIER

🜂 Advanced Rule

Bonfire character are not meant to stand alone and this goes double for the Soldier, who work the best with others. They’re able to shadow others around the battlefield and really focus on being good at one or two weapons.

HEALTH DIE

d8

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Soldiers receive 1 free purchase of the Tactics Skill Suite.

Soldiers receive either the Armor Training (Light) and Shield Training or Armor Training (Medium). On top of this, they gain 1 Weapon Training Mastery for free as well.

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

SMALL UNIT TACTICS Soldiers work as a team. If they or anyone they’re adjacent uses a tactic that moves them (Shift, Step Back, etc.), the Soldier or their adjacent ally may also use that Tactic with no penalties.

IMPROVED MASTERIES Soldiers rely on others to make up for their weaknesses, as such, that gives them greater freedom to really specialize in what they’re good at. Soldiers can purchase Tier 1 - 2 Weapon Masteries for half cost.

WARRIOR TRAINING A Soldier receives a free Tier 1 Weapon Mastery for every 6 levels he’s obtained.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Warrior Training lowered to every 4 levels.

MAGICAL Warrior Training lowered to every 5 levels.

SERVANT Warrior Training lowered to every 3 levels.

WARRIOR Warrior Training lowered to every 3 levels.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

A Soldier focuses so much time drilling with his preferred weapon that he can often forget that principles with one weapon can apply to another. All Tier 4 and above Weapon Masteries are 2 CrP more expensive.

THUG

The Thug is designed to hit hard and make each strike count. As such they have abilities that remove an enemy’s damage reduction and increase the chance to knock someone out.

HEALTH DIE

d6

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Thugs receive 2 free purchase of the Streetwise Skill Suite.

Thugs receive the Armor Training (Light) Mastery and 1 Weapon Training for free.

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

CRITICAL STRIKE If you deal enough damage to move your opponent into the next Wound Category, you trigger a Trauma check vs their full Con.

PRECISION ATTACK The Thug is an expert at making every attack count, he lowers the DR of his opponent by 1 per 6 levels. For /d DR, this is subtracted from the final total.

Example, a level 12 Thug with a dagger (3 dice) vs Scale (2/D), would reduce the enemy’s DR to 4 (6 total DR - 2 from Precision Attack), while one using a club (1 dice) would reduce it to 0 (2 total DR - 2 from Precision Attack).

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT Precision Attack lowered to every 4 levels.

MAGICAL Precision Attack lowered to every 5 levels.

SERVANT Precision Attack lowered to every 3 levels.

WARRIOR Precision Attack lowered to every 3 levels.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

The Thug never really rises above his street rat origins: the Leadership, Administration, Rally, and Recruiting Advanced Skills all cost double.

VANGUARD

The Vanguard is the first in the fray, usually engaging multiple enemies at the same time and trying to do as much damage as possible to soften them up for their allies to follow.

HEALTH DIE

d10

SKILLS & MASTERIES

Vanguards receive 1 free purchase of the Tactics Skill Suite.

They also receive the Armor Training (Medium) Training and 1 Weapon Training for free.

ARCHETYPE ABILITIES

AGAINST THEM ALL When they’re in the thick of it is when the Vanguard thrives. The Vanguard gains their Honor Dice to their Attack rolls against 1 opponent when engaged with 2 or more enemies.

IN & OUT & AROUND Knowing how to slip and out of your opponent’s guards allow the Vanguard to get in and out of a jam quickly.

Once at first level and an additional time for every 8 levels they’ve obtained per combat, the Vanguard may change their engagement zone with one opponent, without any associated penalties.

So, if they’re Threatening their opponent, they could move to Engaged or out to Not Engaged without giving their enemy that free attack.

Additionally, if their enemy is In Their Guard, the Vanguard can move them back to Engaged.

CROWD CONTROL Surrounded by enemies, the Vanguard learns to use the crowd against themselves, forcing his enemies to get in each other’s way.

A Vanguard can use his full defense against an additional opponent if the two opponents are adjacent to each other

FIRST STRIKE The Vanguard hits hard and hits first. Or at least, they try to.

For the first enemy they become Engaged with in a combat, their Measure is increased by 1 ft + 1 per every 5 levels they’ve obtained for determining who attacks first.

So, a level 16 Vanguard wielding a Stiletto (Measure 1.5 ft) who engages with an enemy wielding a Rapier (Measure 4 ft), would have both their attacks go off on the same second but the Vanguard’s attack would resolve first, allowing him to possibly down his enemy before they can strike back.

After that, combat resumes as normal, so he wouldn’t be able to attack the Rapier wielder until he got In Their Guard.

PRIMARY BOON

EXPERT In & Out & Around is lowered to every 5 levels

MAGICAL In & Out & Around is lowered to every 6 levels

SERVANT In & Out & Around is lowered to every 6 levels and First Strike is lowered to every 4 levels.

WARRIOR In & Out & Around is lowered to every 5 levels and First Strike is lowered to every 3 levels.

PRIMARY DRAWBACK

Vanguards are a position of Honor and they don’t take charging into battle lightly. A Vanguard loses 1 Honor for every battle they’re not the first to hit an enemy in.
